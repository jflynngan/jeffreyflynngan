travis amiel is a multidisciplinary artist

Hello, my name is Travis Amiel. I'm a theatre studies major at Emerson College, graduating in 2018. I work at HowlRound Theatre Commons and Emerson's IT Help Desk, I sometimes write/edit Wikipedia articles about theatre, and productively procrastinate.

I'm drawn towards authetic failure, wild experimentation, remixing, and making people laugh.

Most of my art is rooted in performance, as I play with other mediums: painting, collage, sound, video, puppetry, and whatever has compelled me recently.

RIGHT NOW: I'm taking courses in performance art, Viewpoints, Playmaking, training in Butoh, and making a lot of art.
