travis amiel is a multidisciplinary artist

Hello, my name is Travis Amiel I'm a senior theatre studies major at Emerson College. I work at HowlRound Theatre Commons, Emerson's IT Help Desk, sometimes write/edit Wikipedia, and productively procrastinate.

I'm drawn towards authetic failure, wild experimentation, remixing, and making people laugh.

Most of my work is rooted in performance. I've play with other mediums often: acrylic painting, collage, sound, video, and have been photoshopping randomly since my grandmother taught me how.
